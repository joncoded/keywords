Welcome to **Keywords**, your AI-powered language learning companion!

## What Can You Do Here?

### Analyze Phrases
Got a word or phrase you don't understand? Just enter it and get:
- Etymology and origin
- Examples of usage
- Synonyms and related terms
- Inflection tables

### Analyze Writing
Submit a writing sample and discover:
- Difficult vocabulary identified by CEFR level
- Simple explanations for complex words
- Examples and rephrasing suggestions

## Multilingual Support

Keywords works with text in any language! We support:
- Latin alphabets (English, Spanish, French, etc.)
- Cyrillic (Russian, Ukrainian, etc.)
- Chinese, Japanese, Korean
- Arabic, Hebrew, Greek, and more!

## Getting Started

Head to the [home page](/) and try analyzing a phrase or some text. The AI will help you understand it better!
